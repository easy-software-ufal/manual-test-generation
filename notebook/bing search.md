### Test Case 1: Verify Search Functionality

| Step | Actions                      | Verifications                 |
|------|------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| 1    | Open the Bing homepage       | Verify the Bing homepage loads with a search bar visible.  |
| 2    | Enter a query in the search bar (e.g., "weather") | Verify the query is entered correctly in the search bar. |
| 3    | Click on the search icon     | Verify the search results page is displayed with relevant results according to the query.   |

### Test Case 2: Verify News Carousel Functionality

| Step | Actions                      | Verifications                 |
|------|------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| 1    | Open the Bing homepage       | Verify the Bing homepage loads with a news carousel visible below the search bar. |

| 3    | Click on a news item in the carousel | Verify the selected news item opens in a new tab or window.   |
